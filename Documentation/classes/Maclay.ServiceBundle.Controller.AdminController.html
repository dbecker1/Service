removeLineFromLastNameAction()

removeLineFromLastNameAction()

The method for removing the blank spaces of user's last names in the database.

When the users were first imported in to the system, they randomly had new lines before their last names. This affected the ability to search for a user by their last name. This method gets all of the users in the database, removes the spaces at the beginning and end of their last name, and then save it to the database.
